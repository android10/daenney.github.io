Puppet Module Triage

19 February 2015 on puppetlabs, modules, apt, nodejs, hulk smash, triage

Today we had our usual Puppet Module triage. It’s a time a week where module contributors and the Puppetlabs Module Team gather online to discuss, comment, merge or reject PR’s against the different module. This is the best time for you as a contributor to join if you have a PR pending and want feedback.

For the first time we’ve kept minutes of the meeting, what we did and why and you can read up on them here. The Puppet Core Team also keeps their minutes in the same repository: puppet-community/community-triage so feel free to read up on those too.

Two major things happend:

  • hulk_smash got merged into apt/next

  • a huge rework/refactor PR on nodejs landed
